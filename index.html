Features

Everything you need to find your spot.

Live Occupancy

AI-powered cameras track how many people are in each room in real time, so you always know before you go.

Instant Reservations

Book a room for yourself or your group in seconds. No forms, no emails, no waiting for approval.

Group Study

Invite friends to your reservation by their BU email. Everyone gets a Google Calendar invite automatically.

Smart Notifications

Get SMS reminders before your reservation so you never miss a booking or lose your spot.

BU-Only Access

Secured with Google Sign-In restricted to @bu.edu accounts and two-factor authentication.

Calendar Sync

Every reservation syncs directly to Google Calendar for you and all your group members instantly.

How it works

From zero to reserved in four steps.

1

Sign in with BU

Log in securely with your @bu.edu Google account — no new passwords needed.

2

Browse buildings

See which rooms are available right now across BU buildings with live occupancy counts.

3

Make a reservation

Pick your room, set your time, and invite group members if it's a group session.

4

Show up & study

Your reservation is in your calendar. You get a reminder. Just show up.
